How GPA Is Calculated

GPA is calculated by multiplying grade points with the number of subjects received in each grade, and dividing by total subjects.

GPA = (O×10 + A+×9 + A×8 + B+×7 + B×6 + C×5)
/ Total Subjects

How CGPA Is Calculated

CGPA represents your overall academic performance across all completed semesters taking an average.

CGPA = (Sem1 + Sem2 + ... + Sem8) / 8
